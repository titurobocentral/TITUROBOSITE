    About TITUROBO

    Discover the story behind the robots, the people who build them, and the values that drive our success.

    Our Mission

    TITUROBO is dedicated to inspiring and empowering students to pursue careers in Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics . Through competitive robotics, we build not just robots, but problem-solvers, innovators, and leaders.

    Founded in 2018, our team competes in the FIRST Robotics Competition, combining engineering excellence with a spirit of gracious professionalism and a passion for innovation.

    We believe in learning by doing, failing forward, and building a community where every member can contribute to something larger than themselves.

    Core Values

    Ingenuity

    Being able to take ussles scraps and make robots out of them

    Teamwork

    We succeed through collaboration, mutual respect, and leveraging the unique strengths of every team member.

    Excellence

    We strive for excellence in everything we do—from code to mechanics to community outreach and mentorship.

    Meet the Team

    TITUROBO is made up of passionate students, dedicated mentors, and supportive families. Together, we create an environment where innovation thrives and everyone can make a meaningful contribution.
